Pratte-Buehrig Reception

A member of one of our first families, Kara Pratte tied the knot with Britton Buehrig at the Guibourd-Valle House and celebrated with us at our Jean-Baptiste Valle House Gardens. The bride looked stunning and the groom dashing, and we were even able to capture a photo of Kara with her forefather, Jean-Baptiste Sebastien Pratte!
